Security Policy
Reporting a Vulnerability

The security of CiPhyxs is a top priority for DriveBy-Studios.

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in this project, please do not open a public issue. Instead, please use GitHub's Private Vulnerability Reporting feature.

    Navigate to the Security tab in the repository.

    Click the "Report a vulnerability" button.

    Provide a description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and the potential impact.

Our team will review your report and respond as soon as possible. We appreciate your efforts to keep CiPhyxs secure.
